## Getting Started

### Registration

1. Go to http://localhost:3000 (or your deployed URL)
2. Click **Register**
3. Enter:
   - **Email** (used to log in)
   - **Password** (min 6 characters, keep it secure!)
   - **Display Name** (how others see your name in the app)
   - **Native Language** (English will be easier if we understand your language)
4. Click **Register** → You'll be redirected to login
5. Enter your email and password → Click **Login**

### First Login

After logging in, you'll see your **Dashboard**. This is your hub for all learning activities.

## 🗣️ AI Chat Tutor

The AI Chat Tutor is your personal English conversation partner.

### Starting a New Chat

1. Click **Chat** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Conversation**
3. Type your message in English (don't worry about perfection—that's the point!)
4. Hit **Send**

**What happens:**

- The AI responds in real-time (you'll see it typing)
- AI adapts to your proficiency level:
  - **A1-A2** users get simpler vocabulary and shorter responses
  - **B1-B2** users get natural conversation with some idioms
  - **C1-C2** users get advanced nuance and colloquial language
- All messages are saved automatically

### Features

**Grammar Corrections:**

- The AI notices grammatical errors in your messages
- It gently corrects them and explains why
- Example:
  - You: "I go to the store yesterday"
  - AI: "Great sentence! Small note: past tense uses 'went,' not 'go.' You'd say: _'I went to the store yesterday.'_"

**Native Language Support:**

- If you don't understand a response, the AI can explain concepts in your native language
- Just ask: _"Can you explain that in Spanish?"_

**Conversation Topics:**

- Talk about anything: hobbies, daily life, current events, technology
- The AI steers conversations to be educational (teaching new vocab, correcting common mistakes)
- Tip: Ask open-ended questions to practice more

### Tips for Success

- ✅ **Be honest about mistakes** - That's how you learn
- ✅ **Ask follow-up questions** - Keeps the conversation natural
- ✅ **Try new vocabulary** - The AI will gently correct you
- ✅ **Practice regularly** - 10-15 min daily beats 2 hours once a week
- ❌ **Don't use a translator for everything** - Challenge yourself!
- ❌ **Don't ask for sentence-by-sentence translations** - Ask the AI to explain ideas instead

## ✍️ Writing Analysis

Submit any English text and get professional feedback with scoring.

### How to Use

1. Click **Writing** in the sidebar
2. Paste or type your English text (essays, emails, messages, etc.)
3. Click **Analyze**
4. Wait for AI feedback (usually < 5 seconds)

### What You Get

**Score (0-100):**

- **90-100**: Excellent - Near-native writing
- **70-89**: Good - Mostly correct with minor errors
- **50-69**: Fair - Some grammar/structure issues
- **30-49**: Needs Work - Multiple errors affecting clarity
- **0-29**: Very Poor - Significant issues requiring rewrite

**Feedback Includes:**

- ✅ **Grammar errors** - Specific corrections with explanations
- ✅ **Style suggestions** - Clarity, tone, word choice improvements
- ✅ **Structure notes** - Paragraph flow, organization
- ✅ **Vocabulary tips** - Better word choices or phrases

### Example

**Your Text:**

> "I am learning English since 5 years. It's very difficult but I enjoy to practice every day."

**AI Feedback:**

- ❌ _"I am learning... since 5 years"_ → **✅** _"I have been learning English for 5 years"_ (present perfect for ongoing action)
- ❌ _"I enjoy to practice"_ → **✅** _"I enjoy practicing"_ (enjoy + -ing form)
- ✅ Good energy and honesty in your writing!
- 📝 **Score: 72** (Good - grammatically mostly sound, minor tense issues)

### Tips for Better Scores

1. **Proofread first** - Fix obvious typos before submitting
2. **Focus on one tense at a time** - Master present, then past, then future
3. **Re-submit improvements** - Write an improved version and submit again to track progress
4. **Study corrections** - Don't just read them; rewrite them in a notebook
5. **Vary sentence structure** - Mix short and long sentences for better scores

## 📖 Reading Practice

Improve comprehension with passages matched to your proficiency level.

### How to Use

1. Click **Reading** in the sidebar
2. A passage will load automatically for your CEFR level
3. **Read the passage** (no time limit)
4. **Answer 3 comprehension questions**
5. Click **Submit** to grade

### Question Types

Reading questions include:

- ✅ **Multiple choice** - Pick the correct answer
- ✅ **Detail questions** - "According to the passage, what did X do?"
- ✅ **Inference** - "What can you infer about the author's opinion?"

### Passages by Level

| Level   | Example Topics                              | Word Count | Difficulty                               |
| ------- | ------------------------------------------- | ---------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **A1**  | Daily routines, family, simple stories      | 150-200    | Very simple vocab, present tense         |
| **A2**  | Travel, hobbies, basic events               | 200-250    | Simple past, basic conditionals          |
| **B1**  | Culture, technology, social issues          | 250-350    | Mixed tenses, idioms, complex sentences  |
| **B2**  | Current events, abstract topics, literature | 300-400    | Advanced structures, figurative language |
| **C1+** | Philosophical, technical, literary          | 350-400+   | Native-level complexity, rare vocab      |

### Tips for Success

- 📝 **Take notes** while reading - Underline key ideas
- ❓ **Guess unknown words from context** - Don't stop to translate every word
- ✏️ **Try without dictionary first** - Builds inference skills
- 🔄 **Re-read for details** - Before answering comprehension questions
- 💪 **Don't get discouraged** - If you score < 70%, the passage was too hard; try an easier level

## 📚 Vocabulary Tracker

Build your personal vocabulary database.

### Saving Words

**From Chat or Reading:**

1. When you see an unfamiliar word, click **Save to Vocabulary**
2. Enter:
   - **Word** - The English word
   - **Context** - How it was used (example sentence)
   - **Translation** - Definition in your native language

**Manual Entry:**

1. Click **Vocabulary** in sidebar
2. Click **Add New Word**
3. Fill in the same fields

### Example Entry

| Field           | Value                                                             |
| --------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Word**        | Eloquent                                                          |
| **Context**     | "The politician gave an eloquent speech that moved the audience." |
| **Translation** | Expresivo, elocuente (Spanish)                                    |

### Features

- ✅ **View all saved words** in a searchable list
- ✅ **Edit or delete** entries anytime
- ✅ **Organize by theme** (optional - tag words as "Business," "Slang," etc.)
- ✅ **Review regularly** - Spaced repetition helps memory

### Learning Strategy

1. **Save during study** - When you encounter new words
2. **Review daily** - 5-10 minutes going through your list
3. **Use in conversation** - Try new words in your next AI chat
4. **Delete when mastered** - To focus on words you still need to learn

## 💡 Learning Tips

### 🎯 Set Goals

**Example Weekly Goals:**

- ✅ 3 chat sessions (15 min each)
- ✅ 2 writing submissions
- ✅ 1 reading comprehension exercise
- ✅ 10 new vocabulary words

### 🔁 Spaced Repetition

Learn and review on a schedule:

- **Day 1**: Learn word
- **Day 3**: Review word
- **Week 1**: Use in sentence
- **Month 1**: Teach someone else

### 🗣️ Speak Out Loud

When chatting with the AI:

- Pause and read responses aloud
- Practice pronunciation (FluentAI focuses on written English currently)
- Record yourself and listen back

### 📖 Read English Content

Supplement FluentAI with:

- News articles (BBC Learning English, VoiceofAmerica)
- Blog posts in your interest area
- Simplified books (Graded Readers)
- YouTube videos with subtitles

### ✏️ Write Regularly

- Keep an English journal (submit excerpts for analysis)
- Write emails to English-speaking friends
- Comment on online forums
- Submit to Writing Analysis for feedback

### 🤝 Get a Language Partner

- Find tandem partners (Tandem, ConversationExchange apps)
- Practice real conversations while AI gives you feedback on written reflection

## ❓ FAQ

### Can I use FluentAI offline?

No, FluentAI requires an internet connection to connect to the AI and database.

### Is my data private?

✅ Yes. Your chats, writings, and vocabulary are stored in your private account. Only you can see them (unless you share).

### How often should I study?

**Best results: 15-30 min daily**. Consistency beats intensity. 5 min daily > 2 hours once a week.

### What's the best proficiency level to start with?

Take the [Cambridge English online placement test](https://www.cambridgeenglish.org/find-your-level/) to determine your honest level. If unsure, start with **B1** (Intermediate) and adjust down if too hard.

### Can I improve from A1 to B2 in 3 months?

**Realistically**: Possible if you study 1+ hour daily + immerse in English content. More realistic: 6-12 months depending on starting point.

### Why am I getting low writing scores?

Common reasons:

- ❌ Using a translator (loses natural phrasing)
- ❌ Writing too long texts with complex sentences (start simple!)
- ❌ Mixing tenses (stick to one tense per paragraph)
- ❌ Not proofreading before submitting

**Solution**: Write shorter texts (5-8 sentences), proofread, submit frequently.

### Can I delete my account?

Yes, contact support or ask through the app. All data will be permanently deleted.

### Why is the AI giving different responses each time?

AI models are non-deterministic—they generate variations. This is good! It keeps conversations natural. Feedback quality is consistent.

### What if I find a word in a passage that contradicts the "correct" answer?

This rarely happens, but if you find an error, reach out to support with the passage + your concern. We'll review and may adjust.

### Can I export my chat history or vocabulary?

Currently no, but it's on the roadmap! For now, you can manually copy text.

### Is the AI tutor replacing a real teacher?

No. FluentAI is a **supplement**:

- ✅ Great for daily practice, feedback, vocabulary
- ❌ Doesn't replace structured learning (grammar books, courses)
- ❌ Doesn't replace native speaker interaction

**Ideal combo**: FluentAI + online course + conversation partner + immersion
